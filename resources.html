Common questions

How do I start a safety plan?

Begin by identifying your child's specific needs and wandering triggers. Document emergency contacts, medical information, and communication strategies. Our Safety Toolkit provides step-by-step templates you can customize for your family. Contact us for guidance

What should I share with neighbors or school staff?

Share key information about your child's communication style, sensory sensitivities, and any behaviors that might indicate distress. Provide a recent photo and safe return instructions. Our Community Awareness Guide includes printable cards and talking points. Contact us for guidance

How can I support a family in my community?

Educate yourself about autism and wandering prevention. Offer to help create safety plans, attend IEP meetings, or simply be a compassionate listener. Consider volunteering with local autism organizations or joining awareness campaigns. Contact us for guidance

How do donations help?

Your contributions fund free safety resources for families, first responder training programs, research initiatives, and advocacy efforts. Every dollar helps us reach more families with life-saving autism support services and prevention tools. Contact us for guidance

Still not sure where to begin?

We understand that navigating autism support services for families can feel overwhelming. Our team is here to help guide you to the right resources for your unique situation. Whether you're looking for safety information, educational materials, or community connections, we can help route your request to the area that best meets your needs.
